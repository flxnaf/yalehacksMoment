### Best Use of Viam
**Sponsor:** VIAM

Build robots and physical systems without low-level infrastructure headaches. Viam is an open-source platform for configuring, controlling, and programming any hardware: Raspberry Pis, robotic arms, sensors, and more. It handles device communication, data pipelines and ML deployment so you can focus on what your machine actually does.

Hardware lending: Raspberry Pis, SO-101 robotic arms, motors, actuators.

**Looking for:**
- A working hardware project using Viam as its core control layer
- Creative use of sensors, actuators, or ML to solve a real problem
- Bonus: multiple parts of the platform together (vision + navigation, data capture + ML, fleet coordination, etc.)

| Prize |
|-------|
| Viam Rover 2 for each member of the winning team |
